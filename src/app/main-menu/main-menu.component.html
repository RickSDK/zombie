Zombiegeddon Film

Hi! We are a group of highly skilled, highly motivated Seattle based film makers and actors, working hard to create an entertaining horror/comedy zombie movie using local talent.

This movie is going to have it all! Scary scenes, comedy, romance, drama, mystery, action and of course, above all else... zombies!

Help us support this mission by contributing to this project. Our goal is to showcase the talents of the local Seattle film industry and create an awesome movie that is fun to watch and rewarding for all those involved.

Film Synopsis

Strange mysteries are enveloping Seattle, as unusually bloody crime scenes begin popping up with ever greater frequency. Compounding the chaos is the discovery of a new and deadly virus that is turning its victims into zombies.

Eight strangers find their lives intersecting as they fight to stay alive while searching for answers, only to discover the origins of the pandemic are more frightening than anyone could have imagined.

Humanity faces its greatest challenge yet as it comes to grips with the possibility of total annihilation.
